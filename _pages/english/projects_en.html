OneFlow Jekyll Theme

Jekyll is my preferred static website generator and forms the basis for many of my projects. I developed the OneFlow Jekyll Theme to provide an appealing and easy-to-navigate foundation for one-pager websites. Whether you're a web developer, business owner, or individual, this theme offers you a free and simple way to create individually designed one-pagers. The OneFlow Jekyll Theme is my contribution to the open-source community. It stands out for its modern aesthetics and clear structure, making it suitable for both creative presentations and informative business pages.
